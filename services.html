Our Services

TeleMed Solution offers a range of telemedicine services for patients, doctors, and administrators, providing seamless access to healthcare remotely.

For Patients

  • Appointment Booking: Schedule consultations with doctors.
  • Medical Records Access: View your health history and prescriptions.
  • Virtual Consultations: Connect with doctors via video or chat.
  • Prescription Management: Receive and manage digital prescriptions.
  • Health Notifications: Get reminders for appointments and medication.

For Doctors

  • Appointment Management: Organize and manage patient appointments.
  • Consultation Notes: Record and store patient notes.
  • Virtual Consultations: Offer remote consultations via video.
  • Prescription Writing: Send digital prescriptions to patients.
  • Patient History Access: Review medical records for informed care.

For Admins

  • User Management: Manage doctor and patient profiles.
  • Appointment Monitoring: Track and manage all appointments.
  • System Analytics: Access platform usage data.
  • Data Management: Ensure secure handling of user data.
  • Platform Customization: Adjust settings to meet user needs.

Why Choose TeleMed Solution?

  • Convenient, secure, and reliable healthcare access anytime, anywhere.
  • Connect with qualified doctors and manage healthcare efficiently.
